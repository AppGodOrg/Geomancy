Welcome to Geomancy

Geomancy ("earth divination") is a method of divination that interprets a series of 16 figures formed by a randomized process.

A little bit of history

Geomancy was one of the most popular forms of divination throughout Africa and Europe, particularly during the Middle Ages and the Renaissance. In Renaissance magic, geomancy was classified as a "forbidden art."

Using this app

This app makes interpreting a geomantic divination easy. All you need to do is click "Create" and the complex process of forming the divination will be done for you.
